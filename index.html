Originally founded in 2010

BINH BFC are created with a goal to make beautiful and durable outdoor furniture. Our strength is to mix and match different medium such as teak, aluminum, rope, resin wicker and rope to achieve the desirable aesthetic that customers require.

We have a total of 370 employees with rich experiences in metalworking, woodworking, and weaving. Total factory area is around 28,000 m².

Materials

Teak

Our teak furniture is crafted from premium, FSC-certified teak sourced from Costa Rica and Brazil. Each piece is carefully selected and finished to the highest standards.

Materials

Aluminum

Our aluminum furniture is expertly crafted in-house using advanced semi-automatic processes, ensuring exceptional precision, remarkable durability, and enduring performance in any outdoor setting.

Materials

Rope

Our ropes are made from premium polypropylene fibers, engineered to meet the standards for outdoor living. UV-, mold-, and weather-resistant, they combine lasting strength with refined beauty. Paired with teak or aluminum, the rope creates sophisticated, modern furniture that blends durability with timeless style.

Materials

Resin Wicker

Our polyethylene resin wicker is fully weather-resistant and engineered for outdoor living. Each weave is refined through advanced extrusion and oven-baking techniques, ensuring high colorfastness, resilience, and UV protection. The result is wicker that retains its beauty and strength, season after season.
